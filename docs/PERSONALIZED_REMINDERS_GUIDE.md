# 🎯 Personalized Smart Reminders - Hindi/Hinglish Guide

## What's New?

The Smart Reminders system now sends **personalized, conversational messages in Hindi/Hinglish** that feel like a friend reminding you about your crop!

## 🎯 How to Use

### 1. Set Your Name
- Go to Smart Reminders page
- Click "Edit" next to your name
- Enter your name (e.g., "Sumit")
- Click "Save"
- Now all reminders will address you by name!

### 2. Add Your Crop
- Click "Add Crop" button
- Select crop (e.g., moong)
- Enter planting date (today or when you planted)
- Enter field name and area
- Click "Add Crop & Create Schedule"

### 3. View Personalized Reminders
- Click on your crop
- Go to "📋 Reminders" tab
- See all tasks with personalized Hindi messages
- Each message includes:
  - Your name
  - Crop name in Hindi
  - Days since planting
  - Specific action to take

### 4. Listen to Reminders
- Click "🔊 Speak" button
- Browser reads the reminder aloud in Hindi/English
- Perfect for hands-free operation while working

### 5. Mark Tasks Complete
- Click "✓ Done" when you complete a task
- Optionally add notes about what you did
- Progress bar updates automatically

```

  "farmer_name": "Sumit"
```
